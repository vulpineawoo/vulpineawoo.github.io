Code of Conduct

be nice

channel ops can usually administrate their channels how they want to, but in general just assume good intentions and have good intentions

follow the law

vulpineawoo is based in the united states, and as such has to follow laws from there

no bigotry

racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, ableism, etc are not tolerated

respect people's pronouns, and if you do not know them, default to neutral ones such as they/them

no doxxing

avoid sharing/collecting non-public information about people without their consent

additionally, privacy should be expected by default. if this is not the case, for example if a channel is being logged to a website, it should be mentioned where new users can easily see, such as the channel topic. do not share chatlogs without consent
